Feature Keys

Expanding Sword Mechanics: The more you slash, the bigger the sword – use it wisely or get tangled!

Charming Narrative: Follow Shep and Swordie as they bicker, bond, and battle through an emotional tale.

Intuitive Controls: Tap to slash, hold to roll, and swipe to unleash special techniques.

Inventive Puzzles: Use sword length to flip switches, reach keys, and carve paths across terrain.

Exploration & Secrets: Hidden areas, locked shrines, and silly side-quests await the curious.

Family-Friendly Fun: No gore or stress – just light-hearted, skillful fun for players of all ages.
