Slash Quest

Wield the Talking Sword, Rewrite Destiny!

Step into a whimsical realm where destiny is sharp, and swords have something to say! Slash Quest! is a quirky and charming action-adventure game where a humble shepherd becomes an unlikely hero thanks to a magical talking sword. Slash, roll, and puzzle your way through colorful lands to bring balance back to the Queendom.


Are you ready to master the sword that grows with every swing?

About This Game

Slash Quest! is a delightful blend of action, storytelling, and light puzzle-solving wrapped in a fairy tale setting. As Shep, a clueless but kind-hearted farmhand, you must wield a sentient sword that grows as you slash enemies and solve puzzles. But longer isn’t always better – learn to control your blade, explore mystical lands, and fulfill a forgotten prophecy before the Queendom falls into chaos!

Developed by Noble Power Studios, Slash Quest! delivers a blend of light RPG elements, vibrant visual storytelling, and simple yet clever gameplay that keeps players hooked from the first swing to the last boss.

How to Play

Pick Up the Sword: Begin your journey as Shep and bond with Swordie, the legendary (and chatty) blade.

Slash to Grow: Defeat enemies to increase your sword’s size – but be careful not to lose control!

Solve with Swings: Use your sword to activate levers, knock obstacles, and reveal hidden areas.

Dodge & Roll: Time your rolls to avoid enemy attacks and hazards.

Explore the Queendom: Discover new areas, talk to quirky NPCs, and complete side quests.

Unlock Power-Ups: Gain passive abilities, blade forms, and costumes that affect gameplay.

Face Epic Bosses: Slash, solve, and strategize to defeat larger-than-life foes blocking your path.

F A Q

  • Is Slash Quest! free to play?

    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional cosmetic upgrades and unlockable extras.

  • Do I need to be good at action games to enjoy it?

    Not at all! Controls are easy to learn, and the game offers forgiving checkpoints and accessibility options.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes – the entire game is playable offline after initial download.

  • How long is the game?

    The main adventure takes around 6–8 hours, with optional side quests, secrets, and challenges extending that time.

  • Does the sword really grow forever?

    Sort of! Your sword expands with your combo chain and can become hilariously long — but controlling it gets harder the longer it gets!

  • Will there be updates?

    Yes, we plan seasonal content, bonus levels, and more silly side characters in future patches.
