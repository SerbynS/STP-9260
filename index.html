Slash Quest

Wield the Talking Sword, Rewrite Destiny!

Step into a whimsical realm where destiny is sharp, and swords have something to say! Slash Quest! is a quirky and charming action-adventure game where a humble shepherd becomes an unlikely hero thanks to a magical talking sword. Slash, roll, and puzzle your way through colorful lands to bring balance back to the Queendom.


Are you ready to master the sword that grows with every swing?

F A Q

  • Is Slash Quest! free to play?

    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional cosmetic upgrades and unlockable extras.

  • Do I need to be good at action games to enjoy it?

    Not at all! Controls are easy to learn, and the game offers forgiving checkpoints and accessibility options.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes – the entire game is playable offline after initial download.

  • How long is the game?

    The main adventure takes around 6–8 hours, with optional side quests, secrets, and challenges extending that time.

  • Does the sword really grow forever?

    Sort of! Your sword expands with your combo chain and can become hilariously long — but controlling it gets harder the longer it gets!

  • Will there be updates?

    Yes, we plan seasonal content, bonus levels, and more silly side characters in future patches.
